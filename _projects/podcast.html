Agora é que são elas Podcast

The Challenge

The challenge was to create a Cover for the Agora é que são elas Podcast. The goal was to translate the relaxed spirit of podcast conversations into the illustration. I created the cover concept, and it was inspired by the two hosts and the dynamics of their conversation: a bath of immersion in their ideas, broadcast weekly.

You can listen the podcast on Spotifyor Apple Podcasts
